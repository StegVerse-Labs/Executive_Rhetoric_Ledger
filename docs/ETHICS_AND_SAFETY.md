# Ethics and Safety

This system documents public figures and public policy using reputable sources.

## Prohibited Content
- Doxxing private individuals
- Publishing private addresses, phone numbers, or non-public identifiers
- Speculation presented as fact
- Victim identification where not already public and verified in reputable reporting
- Any attempt to infer or “unredact” protected identities

## Allowed Content
- Court filings and docket metadata
- Public records and official transcripts
- Reputable journalism that cites primary documents
- Network/constraint analysis over *publicly documented* events

## Evidence Discipline
- Separate "confirmed" vs "alleged" vs "inference"
- Maintain an audit trail for every claim
